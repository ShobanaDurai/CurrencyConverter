INDIA

India is a rapidly growing economy located in South Asia.India had a diverse and dynamic economy characterized by agriculture, manufacturing, and services sectors. It was one of the world's largest economies, and it had been experiencing consistent economic growth for several years. The currency of India is the Indian Rupee (INR).

1 USD = 83.19 INR

USA

The United States had one of the largest and most developed economies in the world. Its economy was characterized by a diverse range of industries, including technology, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and more. The United States was known for its entrepreneurial spirit, innovation, and high consumer spending.

EUROPE

Europe is a continent with a diverse collection of economies, and it is home to some of the world's largest and most developed economies.The European Union (EU) was a significant economic bloc within Europe, characterized by a strong focus on manufacturing, services, and a robust internal market. The currency used by many European countries within the Eurozone is the Euro (EUR). However, it's important to note that not all European countries are part of the Eurozone, and some still use their national currencies. The currency of Europe is the Euro (EUR).

1 USD = 0.94 EUR

JAPAN

Japan is a highly developed and advanced economy located in East Asia. Japan was known for its strong industrial base, technological innovation, and global influence in various sectors. The currency of Japan is the Japanese Yen (JPY).

1 USD = 147.71 JPY

SOUTH KOREA

South Korea, officially known as the Republic of Korea, is a highly developed economy located in East Asia.South Korea was recognized for its remarkable economic growth, innovation, and technological advancements. The currency of South Korea is the South Korean Won (KRW).

1 USD = 1326.19 KRW

THAILAND

Thailand, officially known as the Kingdom of Thailand, is an emerging economy located in Southeast Asia. Thailand's economy was characterized by a diverse range of industries, including manufacturing, tourism, agriculture, and services. The currency of Thailand is the Thai Baht (THB).

1 USD = 35.71 THB

CHINA

China's economy was characterized by rapid industrialization, a massive labor force, and significant global trade. The currency of China is the Chinese Yuan Renminbi, often referred to as the Yuan (CNY or RMB).China's economy was diverse, with strengths in manufacturing, technology, and export-oriented industries. It was known for being a hub for global manufacturing, producing a wide range of goods from electronics to textiles.

1 USD = 7.29 CNY

SAUDI ARABIA

Saudi Arabia had a diverse and oil-dependent economy, primarily driven by its vast reserves of petroleum. The country is a major global exporter of oil and plays a significant role in the global energy market. The currency of Saudi Arabia is the Saudi Riyal (SAR).Saudi Arabia had embarked on a program known as Vision 2030, which aimed to diversify its economy away from oil dependency and promote sectors like tourism, entertainment, and technology. The government was also working to attract foreign investment and enhance economic sustainability.

1 USD = 3.75 SAR

SINGAPORE

Singapore had a highly developed and prosperous economy known for its open-market policies, business-friendly environment, and strategic location in Southeast Asia. The currency of Singapore is the Singapore Dollar (SGD).Singapore's economy was characterized by a strong focus on finance, technology, trade, and manufacturing. It served as a major global financial hub, hosting numerous multinational corporations and banks.

1 USD = 1.36 SGD

SWITZERLAND

Switzerland is a small, landlocked country in Central Europe known for its strong and stable economy.The currency of Switzerland is the Swiss Franc (CHF).Switzerland's strong economy, political stability, and commitment to innovation have contributed to its high standard of living and global economic prominence.

1 USD = 0.90 CHF

ICELAND

Iceland is a Nordic island country in the North Atlantic Ocean and the most sparsely populated country in Europe. The capital and largest city is Reykjavík, which is home to over 60% of the country's population and the center of the government, culture, and economy.Iceland is known for its stunning natural scenery, including glaciers, volcanoes, waterfalls, and geysers.The official currency of Iceland is the Icelandic króna (ISK).

1 USD = 137.82 ISK

AUSTRALIA

Australia is one of the most culturally diverse countries in the world. It is home to people from over 200 countries, and over 25% of the population was born overseas. The official language of Australia is English, but there are over 200 other languages spoken in the country.The official currency of Australia is the Australian dollar (AUD).

1 USD = 1.56 AUS
